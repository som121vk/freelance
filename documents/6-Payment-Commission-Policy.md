## 2. üí∞ PAYMENT STRUCTURE FOR FREELANCERS

### 2.1 Payment Models

We offer multiple payment options. Your payment model will be specified in your individual contract:

#### **Option A: Per-Project Payment**
- Fixed amount for each completed project
- Payment upon client approval and project delivery
- Suitable for: Designers, writers, one-time projects

**Example:** Logo design project = ‚Çπ5,000 per project

#### **Option B: Hourly Rate**
- Payment based on tracked hours
- Time tracking required (using tools like Toggl/Clockify)
- Suitable for: Developers, consultants, ongoing tasks

**Example:** Web development = ‚Çπ500 per hour

#### **Option C: Monthly Retainer**
- Fixed monthly payment for dedicated availability
- Minimum work hours commitment required
- Suitable for: Regular team members, dedicated developers

**Example:** Full-stack developer retainer = ‚Çπ40,000 per month

#### **Option D: Revenue Share**
- Percentage of project value received from client
- Higher earning potential
- Suitable for: Senior freelancers, project leads

**Example:** You receive 60% of client payment
