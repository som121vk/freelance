## 1. PURPOSE

This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into to protect confidential and proprietary information that may be disclosed during the course of the professional relationship between the parties.

---

## 2. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

"Confidential Information" includes, but is not limited to:

### 2.1 Business Information
- Client lists, contacts, and details
- Pricing structures and profit margins
- Business strategies and plans
- Marketing and sales strategies
- Financial information

### 2.2 Technical Information
- Source code and software
- Designs, wireframes, and prototypes
- Technical documentation
- Algorithms and methodologies
- Trade secrets and know-how

### 2.3 Project Information
- Project requirements and specifications
- Client briefs and deliverables
- Project timelines and budgets
- Communication between company and clients

### 2.4 Other Information
- Any information marked as "Confidential"
- Any information that a reasonable person would consider confidential
- Information disclosed orally that is confirmed in writing as confidential within 7 days

---

## 3. OBLIGATIONS OF RECEIVING PARTY

The Receiving Party agrees to:

### 3.1 Non-Disclosure
- **NOT disclose** Confidential Information to any third party
- Keep all information strictly confidential
- Use Confidential Information ONLY for the intended business purpose

### 3.2 Protection Measures
- Take reasonable measures to protect Confidential Information
- Use at least the same level of care as for their own confidential information
- Limit access to only those who need to know

### 3.3 Prohibited Actions
- NOT copy or reproduce Confidential Information without permission
- NOT reverse engineer any software or technical materials
- NOT use Confidential Information for personal gain

---

## 4. EXCLUSIONS FROM CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

This agreement does NOT apply to information that:

1. Was publicly available before disclosure
2. Becomes publicly available through no fault of Receiving Party
3. Was already known to Receiving Party before disclosure
4. Is independently developed by Receiving Party
5. Is disclosed with written permission from Disclosing Party
6. Must be disclosed by law or court order (with prior notice to Disclosing Party)

---

## 5. RETURN OF INFORMATION

Upon termination of the relationship or upon request:

- Receiving Party must **return or destroy** all Confidential Information
- Delete all digital copies from devices and cloud storage
- Provide written confirmation of destruction if requested

---

## 6. NO LICENSE OR OWNERSHIP

- This agreement does NOT grant any license or ownership rights
- All Confidential Information remains the property of Disclosing Party
- No rights are transferred except the right to review for business purposes

---

## 7. DURATION OF CONFIDENTIALITY

- Confidentiality obligations continue for **[2/3/5]** years after:
  - Termination of employment/contract, OR
  - Date of disclosure (whichever is later)
- For trade secrets, confidentiality continues indefinitely

---

## 8. CONSEQUENCES OF BREACH

### 8.1 Legal Remedies

If Receiving Party breaches this agreement:
- Disclosing Party may seek injunctive relief
- Receiving Party may be liable for damages
- Receiving Party may be liable for legal costs

### 8.2 Additional Consequences
- Immediate termination of contract
- Loss of all pending payments
- Legal prosecution under Indian law

---

## 9. NON-SOLICITATION (Optional)

The Receiving Party agrees:

- NOT to solicit Company's clients for **[6/12/24]** months after contract end
- NOT to hire or recruit Company's other freelancers/employees
- This applies to clients/employees they learned about through Confidential Information
